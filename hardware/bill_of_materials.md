# 📋 Bill of Materials (BOM)

## Market Context & Procurement Strategy
The build was executed during an extended global period of high demand for RAM, SSD, and GPU components. The procurement strategy focused on high-performance storage (PCIe 4.0) and low-latency DDR5 memory. Significant price fluctuations in Mexico's local and global market influenced the choice of vendors, prioritizing a mix of reliability and convenient shipping for all components.
